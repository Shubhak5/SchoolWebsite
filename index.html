Aloysius chapel

the chapel of St. Aloysius College, is a Christian chapel in Mangalore in the state of Karnataka in southwest India

The St. Aloysius Chapel in Mangalore was built by Jesuit Missionaries in 1880 and its interiors painted by the Italian Jesuit Antonio Moscheni in 1899, during the Mangalore Mission in 1878. The Italian Jesuits played an important role in education, health, and social welfare of the Mangalorean Catholic community
